What do humans see that AI misses?
What does AI see that humans ignore?

Focus: AI-human symbiosis in high-stakes decisions Status: Active research

AI has conquered the quantitative layer. Models parse documents in seconds, flag anomalies, calculate risk scores.

But experts still outperform. The radiologist who escalates a scan the algorithm scored as routine. The deal professional who kills an opportunity the spreadsheet approved. They're reading something the models miss.

We're researching where that gap lives—and publishing everything we learn.

Methodology
Retrospective before predictive. We start with deals where the outcome is known, work backward to what was knowable, then build forward.

Most AI demos show the wins. We publish the failures: what AI confidently asserted that was wrong, what it missed entirely, where human judgment needed to override.

Transparency about limitations builds trust—and better systems.

WeWork Case Study Constraints

Documents: Only materials available before merger close (Oct 2021)
Contamination: LLMs trained on post-failure data; controls documented
